Wade Allbritten started Area Realty in 1974 with a commitment to honesty and integrity in the real estate sales and service business. In 1987 Area Realty joined the Key Associates Network of offices to better serve the clients of Owen County. Key Associates is a Network of rural real estate offices working in Southern Indiana. In 2005 Bill Pursell, long time manager, bought the company from Mr. and Mrs. Allbritten. That same dedication to honesty and integrity in the representation of both buyer and sellers exists today. We have the most experienced agents serving Owen and surrounding counties.
